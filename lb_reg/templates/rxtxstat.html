TX and RX Statistics Registers

tx_stats_clr0x0 Bit
0Set this register to 1 to clear all TX statistics counters.
The IP core clears this bit when all counters are cleared.
Bits
31:1reserved.

0Set this register to 1 to clear all RX statistics counters.
