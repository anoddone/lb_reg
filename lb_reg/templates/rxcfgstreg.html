 rx_pktovrflow_error0x0 36-bit error counter
that collects the number of RX frames that are truncated when a FIFO

0x00FC = Lower 32 bits of the error
counter.
0x00FD = Upper 4 bits of the error counter

To read the counter, read the lower 32 bits

after a read.
